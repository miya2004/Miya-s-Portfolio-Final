Learning goal

The power of asking for a feedback and then reflect on it. For feedback I got several checkpoints in feedpulse and seeked for advice from teachers and classmates. Me and my group made e feedback session end each of us wrote what to improve and what are ours positive things. I learned that is easier and you feel more relief after asking for a feedback and reflect on it because in the end you know that the teachers saw your work and know that you did it and you for sure know that your work is good. If I didn't ask for feedbacks I wouldn't know where am i staying and at what level, I wouldn't know what I lack and what is missing in my portfolio and project.

Reflection

1. Portfolio - I know I could have done better and fulfill all the changes that I was adviced to make from my feedbacks, in my opinion I thought that the documentation won't be taking so much time because most of them i had a pdf file in word already bus some of the learning outcomes took more time. And the project I were a bit lazy at first but really gained motivation at the end and did a great job. My reflection is that I have to go easy on myself sometimes because I thought that the group project I didn't do enough with my team but at the end my teachers really were happy with the final result. So for future I will be more organised too.

2. Group Project - As from the feedback from my team members I got a feedback to be consistent with the design, and that I should speak my voice out more so to improve in future I will learn how to stay consistent with my design.

3. Presentation - The presentation that I made my teachers really liked it and I had fun doing it so in future i hope that I come with more ideas and keep making good presentations

4. Peer Feedback - From the feedback from my team members I am trying to improve everything they said I should and in my portfolio every page is consistent with its design.
